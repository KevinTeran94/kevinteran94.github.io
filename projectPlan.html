Executive Summary

Objective Objective The objective is to create a mobile application that will be easy to use and attract potential customers. Customers will be able to choose and purchase one of many subscription plans. The application will also include lots of information on tea for customers. The data gathered will be used to create analytics to help the management team understand the current market. The admin side will be able to view user profiles, manage inventory/subscription plans, etc.
Corporate Goals Addressed Corporate Goals Addressed Better understanding of the market, easier to reach customers, better management of inventory, and new subscription plans offered.
Planned Start Date September 23, 2019
Planned End Date March 27, 2020

Assumptions

This project makes the following assumptions:

  • Project team member will remain in constant contact and are available as needed to complete project tasks and objectives.
  • Employees of the company are willing to embrace the application.
  • The company will be able to correctly implement their database into the application.
  • The company will be able to set up the proper distribution chain for the product.
  • Application will be developed as a mobile app.
  • Employees will cooperate with the project team and deliver required information.

Dependencies

The following are the internal and external dependencies that will have to be acknowledged and addressed:

Internal dependencies:

  • Technology: There is a limitation of knowledge about technology that the team can use to build the application. However, the team knows how to further their understandings of programming, research and keep updating new information which they can apply to develop Tea Subscription Box App.
  • As of January 6, 2020 the team has decided to use Android Studio to complete the project.

External dependencies:

  • Competition: when it comes to competition, for the downside, the similar applications that are already available on the market might create challenges for Tea Subscription Box App to grow its market segment. However, for the good side, it motivates the team to create innovation and a better-quality product.
  • Customer demands: customer satisfaction is very crucial for the business survival. Therefore, the application is designed and developed with user-friendly features that serve for the need of the customer.

Risk Management

Potential Risk Severity(H/M/L) Likelihood(H/M/L) Management Strategy
Timeline estimates are inaccurate H: timeline assumes tasks have been given an appropriate time frame M The timeline of tasks will be regularly reviewed by the team
Team is unfamiliar with the chosen technology L: The team has decided to use a technology they are familiar with. L The team is very familiar with the chosen technology and will seek out resources as needed.
Team members will remain committed throughout the project H: Losing a team member will push the work to other members H The project scope may need to be revaluated
Team is knowledgeable regarding tea M: Some team members may be avid tea drinkers L The team will research various teas to create sample content for the application
Tea inventory becomes difficult to acquire L: There are many tea leaves suppliers L The application could be used for a different product
The application could be used for a different product L: tariffs were imposed in the United States L If tariffs were imposed, the company would either need to absorb the extra costs or increase prices
