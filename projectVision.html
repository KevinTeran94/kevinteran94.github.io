Project Vision

Introduction

This document will provide information with regards to the purpose, scope, positioning, stakeholders, system features, assumptions, and constraints of the proposed Tea Subscription Box project

Purpose

The purpose of this Project Vision Document is to provide an overview to the Tea Subscription Box the team wishes to develop. It will provide the necessary ideas and details for the team to develop the project and what the organization can expect from the project at the time of completion.

Scope

In-scope:The project will include the Front-end User interface that the customer will interact with to mange their profile, leave reviews and select their subscription plan. The administrator view will be able to view customer profiles, manager inventory and view analytics generated from the data in the database

This project will not handle human resources, payroll and the legal part of the company. The company will be responsible to handle the shipping and handling part of the business.

Positioning

Business Opportunity

There is a lack of an application that can teach customers about the types of teas in the market and allow them to purchase said teas and tea accessories through a simple to use interface. The organization would also like some analytics based on the customers to better judge the market and position themselves.

Problem Statement

The problem of Lack of analytics based on the data received through business
affects The Tea company interested in this project
the impact of which is The organization does not know where they stand in the market or where the market is moving to.
a successful solution would be Analytics generated from the data gathered from the users of the application

Product Position Statement

For Tea loving customers
Who Want high quality tea and a simple way to get the tea with better price
The Tea Box Subscription App is a subscription plan
That Ships tea and tea accessories (if needed) monthly based on their selected plan
Unlike Competitors who sell their tea through specialty shops, grocery stores or online
Our product Offers a wide variety of high-quality teas delivered monthly at the customers’ requests

Assumptions

Right now, the project is proposed as a mobile application. However, if the technology is not available or the team finds a better technology to complete the project with, the application may change to a web application. Since a real company’s database is not readily available, a sample database will be designed and implemented by the project team. This is the plan going forward unless a stakeholder requires a change.

Constraints

One of the major design constraints is the use of a sample database. Everything will be based on the team’s sample database and thus it may be difficult to implement another database later. However, the implementation of another database after the completion of the project will fall on the Tea company.
